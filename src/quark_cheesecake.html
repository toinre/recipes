Quark Cheescake

Description

Ingredients for 1 Portion:

  • 750g quark (20% fat)
  • 250g mascarpone
  • 5 eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 60g neutral protein powder
  • 250g butter
  • 250g fig-strawberry paste
  • ½ packet baking powder
  • a bit of lemon juice

For the Fig Paste:

  • 150g fresh peeled figs
  • 100ml water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation of the Fig Paste:

  1. Wash and quarter the figs, then place them in a pot with 100ml water and lemon juice.
  2. Simmer over medium heat for 15–20 minutes until the figs are soft, stirring regularly.
  3. Puree the mixture and let it cool.

Preparation of the Cheesecake:

  1. Mix quark, eggs, flour, melted butter, fig paste, baking powder, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  2. 2. Pour the batter into a greased springform pan (26 cm).
  3. 3. Bake at 170°C (top/bottom heat) for 1 hour.
  4. 4. Let it cool in the turned-off oven.
