Tuna Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients for 2 Portions:

  • 250g pasta (spaghetti)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of tuna, without oil
  • 250ml sweet cream
  • Tomato paste
  • Herbs of Provence
  • Salt
  • Oil

Preparation:

  1. Cook the spaghetti al dente.
  2. Dice the onion finely and saut√© it in a pot with some oil, then add the tuna.
  3. Add the cream.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste until the sauce has a nice pink color.
  5. Add plenty of Herbs of Provence and season with salt.
  6. If necessary, add a bit of water to adjust the sauce consistency.

Alternative:

Instead of tuna, you can use zucchini and salmon.
