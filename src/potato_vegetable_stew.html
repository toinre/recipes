Potato Vegetable Stew

Description

Ingredients for 5 Portions:

  • 6 large potatoes
  • 8 large mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • Spicy sausage
  • 2 bell peppers (red and green)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g crushed tomatoes
  • 300ml broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp hot mustard
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½ tsp hot paprika powder

Preparation:

  1. Prepare and sauté the potatoes and vegetables.
  2. Add the tomatoes, broth, and spices, then cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Season to taste and serve.

Time Required:

  • Preparation time: approx. 25 minutes
  • Total time: approx. 50 minutes
  • Difficulty level: normal
  • Calories per serving: approx. 245 kcal
